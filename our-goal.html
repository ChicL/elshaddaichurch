Our Goal is to Worship and Serve

El-Shaddaï Church's Core Values:

Six Commitments to Fulfilling Our Mission and Vision

Worship and Serve The Most High God

We wholeheartedly dedicate ourselves to worshiping and serving God, acknowledging His sovereignty, and following His teachings.

Fulfilling the Great Commission:

In obedience to Jesus Christ's command in Matthew 28:19-20, we actively engage in sharing the Gospel and making disciples of all nations.

Ministering to Members and Others

With compassion and love, we strive to meet the spiritual, emotional, and physical needs of our members and those in the community around us.

Empowered by Faith in Christ

We place our trust in God, relying on His strength and guidance to carry out our mission and achieve our vision.

Unity and Fellowship in Christ

Embracing diversity, we foster a spirit of unity and fellowship among our members, promoting a loving and supportive community.

Stewardship and Responsibility

Recognizing our responsibility as stewards of God's resources, we use them wisely and responsibly to fulfill our purpose effectively.

Our mission

Our mission and evangelism efforts span across Asia, Australia, and New Zealand. We are committed to establishing social facilities and empowering the saints through Biblical schools, mission schools, training seminars, and character-building initiatives. Additionally, we engage in various social works such as constructing nursery schools, dispensaries, and pharmacies, all while establishing a network for evangelism. We strongly encourage every Christian to learn to share the gospel individually, fostering a peaceful climate. Our focus also extends to providing moral counsel, managing world ecology, and training individuals and families in peace reconciliation and rehabilitation.
