We believe...

Affirming Jesus Christ's dual nature as both true God and man, He sacrificially died on the cross to redeem us from sin. Rising bodily from death, He ascended to heaven, assuming a position of honor at the Father's right hand. As our High Priest and Advocate, He intercedes for us.

Statement of Faith in El-Shaddaï

  • Holy Bible as the inspired Word of God

    We believe the Holy Bible as the inspired Word of God, without any error, the all-sufficient authority in matters of faith, doctrine, and Christian living.

    2.Peter 1:21

  • We believe in one God

    We believe in one God, eternally existing in three Persons: The Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit.

  • We believe in the Virgin Birth of Jesus Christ

    We believe in the Virgin Birth of Jesus Christ, and that He is true God and man. He died on the cross for our sins. He rose bodily from the dead, ascended into heaven where He sits at the right band of the Father and is now our High Priest and Advocate. 1.Tim. 3:16.

    1.Tim. 3:16

  • Man was created in the image of God

    We believe that man was created in the image of God but fell into sin and therefore lost. Only through regeneration by the work of the Holy Spirit can man's salvation and spiritual life be obtained.

  • Evangelistic and missionary

    We believe in evangelistic and missionary fervor and endeavor.

    Acts 1:8 Mark 16:15-18

  • We believe that eternal salvation

    We believe that eternal salvation is the free gift of God, entirely apart from man's works, and is possessed by any and all who have faith in and receive Jesus Christ as their personal Lord and Savior.

  • We believe in a regenerated church

    We believe in a regenerated church membership: that is, persons old enough to understand their need for Jesus Christ and, of their own free will, accepted Him as their personal Lord and Savior.

  • We believe that eternal salvation

    We believe that baptism is by immersion in water and the Lord's Supper is a memorial to His suffering and death on the cross. These are ordinances to be observed by the church in its present age. They are, however, not to be regarded as means for man's salvation.

    Matthew 28:19-20 Romans 6:3

  • We believe in the baptism of the Holy Ghost

    We believe in the baptism of the Holy Ghost according to acts 2:4, 10:46, 19:6, and the present ministry of the Spirit in and through the believer as manifest in the ministries, gifts and fruit of the Spirit.

    Acts 2:4 Acts 10:46 Acts 19:6

  • We believe in the glorious return of Jesus Christ

    We believe in the personal, bodily, imminent, and glorious return of the Lord Jesus Christ and that His Second Corming inspires believers for dynamic and zealous life and service for Him while waiting for His return.

  • We believe in the resurrection of the dead

    We believe in the resurrection of the dead, the everlasting blessedness of the saved in heaven, and the everlasting punishment and separation from God of the unsaved in hell.

  • We believe in the autonomy of the local church

    We believe in the autonomy of the local church. The church shall manage its own affairs and shall not be subject to any other religious body or organization.

  • We believe that eternal salvation

    We believe in Holy Matrimony. We believe in the sanctity of marriage. We believe that the scriptural teaching is that monogamy.

    Genesis 2:24 Matt. 5:32 Mark 10:7-9 Romans 7:2 1. Cor. 7:10-11 1. Tim. 3:12 Heb. 13:4

  • We believe in the dedication

    We believe in the dedication of infants. It is our belief that Christian parents should dedicate their children to the Lord.

    1. Sam. 1:24-28 Mark 10:13-16

  • We believe in the fellowship

    We believe in the fellowship of the Lord's supper.

    Matt. 26:26Mark 14:22Luke 22:191. Cor. 10:161. Cor. 11:23

The scripture

Our doctrine supports monogamy as per Scripture's teaching. Embracing Acts 2:4; Acts 10:46; Acts 19:6, we advocate for the baptism of the Holy Ghost. Baptism is through water immersion; the Lord's Supper commemorates His sacrifice. We acknowledge humanity's creation in God's image.

Empowered by the gospel

Our aim is to extend its message of peace to every corner of the world, encompassing households, seniors, adults, youth, and children. Our mission includes evangelizing Africa and conducting missions in Northern Africa, America, and South America.
